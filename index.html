Joss Roman

Institute for Macroeconomic and International Policies (CEPREMAP & PSE)

Welcome!

I am a macroeconomist with research interests in climate change, public policies, finance, and heterogeneities.

I received my PhD in economics from PSL Research University - Paris Dauphine in December 2021. After visiting the Grantham Research Institute at the London School of Economics, I joined the European Commission’s Joint Research Centre where I spent 3 years in the macroeconomic modelling team. I am now a senior researcher at the Paris School of Economics within the Institute for Macroeconomic and International Policies.
